The start of the championship is approaching and for the fantastic coaches it is time to start thinking about their fantastic teams to participate in Fantacalcio 2025/26. There is still more than a month to wait for the next football movements, to start getting to know the teams and the new grafts, but in the meantime the fans will already be starting to create their ideal team. And for all fans of fantasy football, thisyear FcInterNews has decided to join "Fantalgoritmo Bomber", the fantasy game created for all Inter fans in collaboration with Fantalgoritmo and FantaMaster.

The operation is simple: by subscribing to Fantalgoritmo through this link you will take part in a special league dedicated to all Nerazzurri fans: once the registration phase is over and the leagues are created, all subscribers will receive an entry link to FantaMaster where they will take part in the special championship with their team. As in any fantasy football, each Fanta Coach has 270 Fanta Millions (virtual) available to buy the following 25 players for each team: 3 goalkeepers, 8 defenders, 8 midfielders and 6 strikers among those proposed in the published list of players. There is time until September 14, 2025 to join.

At the end of each day of the championship, scores will be assigned in a classic way through players' votes, bonuses and malus. Registration costs 20 euros but there are rich prizes up for grabs. At the end of each day, starting from the 3rd, the team that has scored the most points will be rewarded, for a total of 36 awards. And at the end of the championship a final ranking will be drawn, including the cumulative score of 36 days of the championship. This ranking will see the top 5 winners.

Prize detail

During each day of the Serie A championship included in the competition period, the participant who will get the highest score in the league's daily ranking will add a Good Gift Store.inter.it from the value of €100 VAT included.
