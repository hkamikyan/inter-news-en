Yet, Cesena was also able to respond to this goal, stamping the scoreboard with Galvagno (another successor) for the final 3-3. For Inter, the next appointment will therefore be on Tuesday at 6.30 pm against Cagliari for the Super Cup final. Appointment at the Civic Arena in Milan.
